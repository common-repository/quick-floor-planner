== Description ==

Free Quick Floor Planner lets your visitors draw their own floor plan. 
To use it, just put the shortcode [quick-floor-planner] in one of your pages' content.
Whether you manage a real-estate agency or a construction company website, interior design studio,
renovation company or even an event management business, 
Quick Floor Planner is an easy-to-use plugin that greatly enhance 
the engagement of your visitors on your Wordpress site.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/quick-floor-planner` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. To use it, simply add the shortcode [quick-floor-planner] in one of your pages' content section. This will display a canvas and buttons on your page to let your visitors draw their own floor plan.
